The Problem

Selling land through
a realtor is broken.

Most landowners think they have one option — list with an agent, wait 6–18 months, pay 6–8% in commissions, and hope a buyer's financing doesn't collapse at closing.

That system wasn't built for land sellers. We are.

⏳

Waiting 6–18 months with no guarantee

Vacant land sits on the MLS 3–5× longer than homes. Most listings expire with zero offers while carrying costs pile up.

💸

Losing 6–8% before you see a dime

Commissions, title fees, transfer taxes — you hand over thousands in costs before the proceeds ever reach you.

🏦

Buyers who can't get financed

Banks barely lend on raw land. Most deals collapse because the buyer's loan falls through after months of your time.

🗺️

Carrying costs that bleed you every year

Property taxes, HOA dues, liability insurance. Every year you hold unused land, you're paying for an asset doing nothing for you.

Common Questions

"Will your offer be way below market value?"

−
Our offers are built on real county data and comparable sales for your specific parcel type. We need fair deals or our business model doesn't work. What we can't match is a full retail listing price after 12 months of waiting — but what we do offer is certainty, speed, and cash in hand today.

"I inherited land without the deed — can you still buy it?"

+
Yes. We handle estate situations all the time. Our team and title partners know how to navigate probate, missing documentation, and complex inheritance scenarios. Just send us the parcel details and we'll guide you through exactly what's needed.

"The land has years of back taxes — is it sellable?"

+
Absolutely. We buy tax-delinquent land regularly. In most cases we absorb the back taxes as part of the transaction so you don't come out of pocket before closing. Submit the details and we'll tell you what we can do.

"The land is landlocked — will anyone buy it?"

+
We specifically look for parcels most buyers won't touch. Landlocked, difficult access, no utilities — we know how to work with all of these. It may affect the offer amount, but we'll still evaluate and respond with a number.

"How is this different from listing with a realtor?"

+
A realtor lists your land and waits for a buyer to appear — that takes 6–18 months and most land listings expire without selling. We are the buyer. No listing period, no commissions, no deal collapsing at the last minute because a buyer couldn't get financed.

"Do I need to prepare or fix up the land first?"

+
Nothing at all. We buy land exactly as-is. Don't clear it, don't clean it, don't spend a dollar on it. Just send us the parcel information and we'll handle everything from there.
